The Sport of Wrestling

The oldest sport in the world

The sport of wrestling will challenge you mentally and physically.

Wrestling can teach you many things, such as:

  • Self-discipline
  • Self-defence
  • Self-confidence
  • Sportsmanship
  • Competitiveness
  • Mental Toughness
